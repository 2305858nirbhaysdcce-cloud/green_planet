Our Mission

Green Planet aims to raise awareness about environmental issues and promote sustainable living. Together with local NGOs, we work towards a cleaner, greener future for our planet.

Recent Activities

Beach Cleanup Drive

Over 200 volunteers joined hands to clean the local beach and collected 500 kg of plastic waste.

Tree Plantation Week

Planted over 1,000 saplings in urban and rural areas to promote reforestation.

Eco-Awareness Workshop

Conducted workshops in schools to educate children on recycling and waste management.
